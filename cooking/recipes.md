# creme caramel
caramel
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup water
custard
- 4 eggs
- 25g white chocolate
- 500ml condensed milk
- 200ml water/milk
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
make the caramel
- mix sugar and water in glass bowl
- heat the mixture in low-medium heat for 10 - 12 minutes
- turn heat off immediately when caramelized
- carefully add 2 tablespoons of water to stop the cooking process
make the custard
- mix condensed milk with water/milk and heat to a boiling point
- melt the white chocolate together with the mixture
- mix eggs and vanilla with a fork slowly
- temper the egg with the milk mixture little by little
water bath
- in the same bowl, put the caramel in the bottom and the custard on top
- set the bowl in a hot water bath up to 3/4 of the top rim for at least 30 mins
- afterwards refrigerate for at least 6 hours

# bread
wet
- 1 teaspoon yeast
- 1 cup of water
- 1/2 tablespoon sugar
dry
- 2 cup of bread flour
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 cup of skim milk
dough
- mix dry and wet ingredients
- form a dough and leave around an hour to autolyse
- knead dough until past window pane test
- proof dough until double in size
- bake at 140C for 15 mins (with air fryer)
